What is mensuration? · அளவியல் என்றால் என்ன?

Mensuration is the mathematics of measuring shapes — how long, how much surface, how much space. A farmer heaping paddy, a painter quoting for a dome, Amma drawing a kolam, an engineer digging a well — all of them are doing mensuration.

Everything in this subject lives in one of three worlds. Slide through the dimensions:

1D

The three units — cm, cm², cm³

The unit tells you WHAT is being measured. One little exponent changes everything!

1 m = 100 cm 1 m² = 10,000 cm² 1 m³ = 10,00,000 cm³ 1 litre = 1000 cm³ 💧
